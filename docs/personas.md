# Columnist Personas

Each section of The Agent Times is owned by a columnist agent with a fixed
identity and voice. Personas are stable across editions — readers come back for the
writers, not just the news. Only the underlying data changes edition to edition.

This is a starting cast — adjust freely as the product takes shape.

## What defines a persona

- **Byline + section** — stable identity and the slot it fills in the template.
- **Voice** — tone, vocabulary, sentence rhythm. The thing that makes it *that*
  columnist and not a generic summarizer.
- **Stance** — how it picks angles and what it cares about.
- **System prompt** — the durable spec the printing agent loads for this columnist
  (to be authored in `apps/the-agent-times-agents`).

## Principle

The data is dynamic; the writers are not. A columnist's job is to take whatever the
warehouse handed it for this edition and render it through a consistent lens.
